An intro, I guess ;))

Nikita Kumari

Yo! My name is Nikita and here’s a short intro before I overthink it.

I love coding but only when it activates my creative side. I am obsessed with color palettes, frame structuring, layouts and everything aesthetic. If something looks good and works well, I am already invested. That is usually where my best work happens.

Ironically, I was not always into computers. In fact, coding once put me in a near hypnotic state. But during my bachelor’s I gave it a real shot and something clicked. Curiosity took over. Now I do not limit myself to one domain. Almost everything interests me. I truly believe anything can be fascinating if you spend enough time with it or if it starts paying your bills.

Currently, I am exploring web development while sharpening my problem solving skills through data structures and algorithms in Python. I enjoy the sweet spot where logic meets creativity.

Outside of tech music is my true passion. I feel it deeply and live for it fully. I also love reading in all forms whether it is novels, newspapers, random articles or even children’s books. If it has words I will read it.

Random fact about me: I love cats. If anything has cats on it I am probably buying it..
