Nikita Kumari

Yo! My name is Nikita and here's a short intro before I overthink it.

I love coding, especially when it lets me bring in my creative side. I am obsessed with color palettes, frame structuring, layouts, and all the tiny details that make something feel good to use. If something looks good and works well, I am already invested. That is usually where my best work happens.

Ironically, I was not always into computers. Coding once put me in a near hypnotic state, but during my bachelor's I gave it a real shot and something clicked. Curiosity took over. Now I do not limit myself to one domain. Almost everything interests me, and I like learning until the thing starts making sense.

Currently, I am exploring web development while sharpening my problem solving skills through data structures and algorithms in Python. I enjoy the sweet spot where logic meets creativity.

Outside of tech, music is my true passion. I feel it deeply and live for it fully. I also love reading in all forms, whether it is novels, newspapers, random articles, or even children's books. If it has words, I will probably read it.

For the founder inbox, recruiter tab, or curious-human moment.
